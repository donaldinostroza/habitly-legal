To request deletion of your Habitly account and associated data, please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to **support@joinhabitly.app** from the email address associated with your Habitly account.
2. Include the subject: **Account Deletion Request**.

## What data is deleted
- User account
- Child profiles
- Habits, rewards, and progress data

## Retention period
All data will be permanently deleted within **30 days** of verification.
Backup data may be retained for a limited time for security and legal purposes.
