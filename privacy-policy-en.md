# Privacy Policy – Habitly

**Last updated:** Jan 7, 2026.

## 1. Introduction
Habitly (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a mobile application designed to help parents and legal guardians support and manage healthy habits for their children. We take privacy seriously, especially when it involves children’s data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data when you use Habitly.

By using Habitly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Who Uses Habitly
- Habitly accounts are created and managed exclusively by adults (parents or legal guardians).
- Children do not create independent accounts and do not provide login credentials such as email, passwords, or social sign-in.
- Children may access the application only through profiles created and controlled by an adult.

Parental consent is required before creating or managing a child profile.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information About Adults
We collect the following personal data from adult users:
- Email address

### 3.2 Information About Children (Provided by the Adult)
We may collect and store the following information for child profiles:
- Name or nickname  
- Date of birth  
- Habits and assigned tasks  
- Rewards  
- Progress and completion history  

All child-related data is entered voluntarily by the adult user.

---

## 4. Use of Artificial Intelligence
Habitly uses third-party artificial intelligence services to generate habit plan recommendations.

- Prompts may include behavioral information voluntarily provided by the adult.
- Generated plans and recommendations may be stored to provide ongoing functionality.
- We do not use personal data to train our own AI models.

---

## 5. Analytics and Diagnostics
We use analytics and diagnostics tools to improve the stability and usability of Habitly, including:
- Firebase Analytics
- Firebase Crashlytics
- Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

These tools collect aggregated and statistical usage data. We do not use analytics for advertising or behavioral profiling.

---

## 6. Communications
We may send functional communications, such as:
- Account-related notifications
- Habit reminders
- Service updates

We do not send marketing or promotional communications at this time. If this changes in the future, users will have the ability to opt out.

---

## 7. Data Storage and Security
- Data is stored in Google Cloud Platform data centers located in the **us-central** region.
- Personal data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Access to data is restricted and protected using industry-standard security measures.

---

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion
- If an adult user deletes their account, all associated personal data and child profiles will be permanently deleted within **30 days**.
- Backups and logs are handled securely and deleted within a reasonable technical timeframe.

---

## 9. User Rights
Adult users have the right to:
- Access and update their personal information
- Delete their account and associated child profiles
- Request a copy of their data by contacting support

Data export is not currently available directly in the app, but we will respond to data requests via email.

---

## 10. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

We only share data with service providers strictly necessary to operate the application (such as analytics and infrastructure providers).

---

## 11. Children’s Privacy
Habitly is designed for use by adults managing child profiles.

We comply with applicable children’s privacy regulations, including:
- Chilean data protection laws (current and future)
- COPPA (U.S.) and similar international frameworks

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without parental consent.

---

## 12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date and notify users of material changes.
